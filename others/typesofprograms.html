The philosophy behind our approach

We distill complex concepts to their fundamentals and simplify them so your child can easily understand them. Our lessons involve utilizing the socratic method to inculcate critical thinking as the main driver behind full comprehension of a concept. These are the lessons we believe will intrigue your child to think more about the world around them.

Learning through fun and interactive activities

Our curriculum offers a wide variety of activities. Each specifically catered to nurturing and guiding children through their formative years. Activities like games, discussions and group activities will keep lesson interesting and engaging.
