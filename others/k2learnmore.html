Our Kindergarten 2 Programmes
Give your child the best preparation for P1 education money can buy. Our programs help your child develop skills, confidence and passion required for formal education. We also focus on teaching values that are important to becoming a self directed learner.
Program Outline


English

Duration of class: 2hrs
Class size: 10-12 students
This programme focuses on developing key reading, writing and speaking skills so your child has a smooth transition to primary 1.


Mathematics

Duration of class: 2hrs
Class size: 10-12 students
We prepare your child for primary 1 education by teaching the important concepts and fundamentals that will be covered in Primary 1.


Mother Tongue

Duration of class: 2hrs
Class size: 10-12 students
In Kindergarten 2, we focus on helping your child formulate sentences, expand her use of daily vocabulary while at the same time deepen their passion for the language.
